# Musical-Dataset
This report presents an exploratory analysis of a music dataset containing audio features, metadata, and popularity measures for a wide range of tracks


The scope of this report includes:

Descriptive statistics to summarize distributions, central tendencies, and variability within the key audio features.
Data visualizations, including scatterplots, histograms, grouped bar charts, and correlation heatmaps, to highlight relationships and reveal structural patterns within the dataset.
Trend analysis to examine how musical attributes such as danceability, energy, and valence correspond with popularity, and to assess whether specific sonic qualities are consistently associated with stronger listener engagement.

The outcomes of this analysis aim to provide:

 A clear statistical profile of the dataset’s core musical features.
 Visual evidence of trends that may inform future modeling, prediction tasks, or deeper exploration of listener behavior.

By integrating descriptive statistics with graphical exploration in R, this report seeks to uncover meaningful connections between musical attributes and popularity, offering both technical rigor and interpretive insight into the dynamics shaping contemporary music data.
